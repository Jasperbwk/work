Polaris

A Personal Work Light and Timer
Project Overview
The goal of this team class project was to design a light that could be used in a home environment. We chose to focus on a work/study light that also functions as a timer. Allowing users to better pace out their day by focusing, with both time and light, on the moment. The project timeline was 5 weeks from beginning to end.
Timeframe: 5 weeks

Group project by Jasper Karras, Kai Mizuta and Sage Davis.
