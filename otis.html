Otis

A wooden toy for people of all ages

Sustainable

Made of sustainably harvested wood and natural dyes.

Fun

Smooth sliding and spinning motion with calm acoustics.

Smooth

Rollerballs installed on the underside allow for smooth motion.

Context

The goal of this solo class project was to create a small, engaging wooden toy with moving parts. I chose to create an octopus because I knew it would make a fun toy with interesting movement. The project timeline was 4 weeks from beginning to end.
